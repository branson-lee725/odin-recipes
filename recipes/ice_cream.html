Ice Cream

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2C heavy cream
  • 1-1/2C milk
  • 2/3C sugar
  • 2tsp vanilla
  • 1/8tsp salt

Directions

  1. Combine ingredient in a saucepan until combined and warmed
  2. pour into heatproof bowl and chill for several hous in fridge
  3. remove and whisk well. Churn in ice cream maker for 20-30 min. For firmer ice cream freeze for several hours
